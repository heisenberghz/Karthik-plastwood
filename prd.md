1. Project Overview

Develop a clean, modern, responsive website that establishes an online presence for Karthik Fiber Works and Karthik Plast Wood.

The website should focus on showcasing products, building customer trust, and making it easy for potential customers to contact the business.

The design should feel professional yet simple, avoiding unnecessary animations or complexity.

16. Project Scope

This is a simple static business website focused on showcasing the company's products and services. It does not include user accounts, product purchasing, inventory management, payment gateways, or an admin dashboard. The emphasis is on providing a clean, trustworthy online presence with essential business information, product highlights, and convenient contact options for potential customers.
